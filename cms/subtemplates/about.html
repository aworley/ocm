What is a Pika?

from Webster's Revised Unabridged Dictionary (1913) 
Pika Pi"ka, n. (Zo"ol.)
   Any one of several species of rodents 
   of the genus Lagomys, resembling small
   tailless rabbits.  They inhabit the 
   high mountains of Asia and America. 
   Called also calling hare, and crying 
   hare. See Chief hare.
